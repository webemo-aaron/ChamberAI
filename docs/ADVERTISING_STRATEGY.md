## Referral Program Strategy

**For Referrer**:
- 1 month free Pro for each referred chamber that signs up
- $100 account credit for each referred enterprise customer
- Unlimited referrals

**For Referee**:
- 30% off first 3 months of Pro
- Free 1-on-1 onboarding call
- Priority support

### Distribution

1. **In-app referral button**
   - Post-signup modal
   - Settings page
   - Success email

2. **Email referral program**
   - Monthly to all users
   - Highlight earnings
   - Easy share links

3. **Social sharing**
   - Pre-written tweet/LinkedIn post
   - Trackable links
   - Social proof: "Referred by [name]"

### Incentive Ladder

- 1 referral = 1 month free
- 3 referrals = 3 months free + merchandise
- 5 referrals = Free annual plan + featured story
- 10+ referrals = Custom partnership

# ChamberAI Premium Tier Strategy

## Executive Summary

ChamberAI v1.0.0 ships with a **free tier** that serves individual chambers and small boards. The premium tier strategy targets **"discerning chambers"** — professional governance bodies with budgets of $150-$500/month that demand audit-ready records, legal compliance, and measurable accountability.

This document outlines the 4-tier pricing model, ideal customer profile, market positioning, and unit economics that unlock **$150k+ ARR** by Year 1.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile: Discerning Chambers

**Who They Are:**
- State/local chambers of commerce with 20-200 member organizations
- Professional associations (medical, legal, engineering boards)
- Non-profit boards with audit requirements
- Executive councils in Fortune 500 companies
- Industry groups requiring regulatory compliance (e.g., insurance, finance)

**What They Value:**
1. **Roberts Rules compliance** — governance standards that pass audits
2. **Audit-ready records** — DOCX exports, signed minutes, audit trails
3. **Conflict of interest tracking** — liability reduction
4. **Accountability metrics** — dashboards showing action item completion
5. **Legal defensibility** — immutable version history, approval signatures
6. **Integration with legal/financial systems** — API access, Zapier

**Why They Buy:**
- Executive directors face personal liability for governance failures
- Boards need to prove due diligence to regulators and members
- Non-profit auditors explicitly request governance software
- $200-500/month is negligible for an organization with $1M+ budget

**How Much They Spend:**
- Budget: $2,000-6,000/year governance software
- Decision cycle: 2-4 weeks (demo → pilot → annual contract)
- Procurement: Executive director signs off, often auto-renews

### Tier Definitions

#### Free
- **Use case**: Individual board secretaries, volunteer-run associations
- **Positioning**: "Start governing better immediately"
- **Conversion path**: 3-5% of free users convert to Pro within 6 months
- **LTV**: $0 (churn rate too high)

#### Pro ($29/month)
- **Use case**: Small chambers, startup boards, early adopters
- **Sweet spot**: 5-10 team members
- **Key wins**: Governance report shows boards where they stand; Zapier enables workflow automation
- **ROI talk**: "Saves 5 hours/month on minutes → $120 ROI at $30 cost"
- **Contracts**: 70% monthly, 30% annual
- **LTV**: ~$870 (30-month retention)
- **CAC**: $15-30 (organic + landing page)

#### Council ($149/month) ← **PROFITABILITY ENGINE**
- **Use case**: Regional chambers, professional boards, audit-ready orgs
- **Sweet spot**: 15-30 team members
- **Key wins**: DOCX for legal files + full API + analytics
- **ROI talk**: "Minutes auto-archive to legal system, eliminates audit finding → $2,000+ risk reduction"
- **Contracts**: 20% monthly, 80% annual
- **LTV**: ~$3,576 (24-month retention, 87% annual renewal)
- **CAC**: $300-500 (sales-assisted)
- **Margin**: ~61% after support

#### Network ($399/month)
- **Use case**: Multi-state organizations, national associations, enterprise
- **Sweet spot**: Unlimited members, multiple chambers
- **Key wins**: Dedicated account management, 99.99% SLA, custom integrations
- **ROI talk**: "Central governance dashboard across 50 chambers → operational insight"
- **Contracts**: 5% monthly, 95% annual (3-year bulk contracts)
- **LTV**: ~$11,424 (24-month retention)
- **CAC**: $1,500+ (enterprise sales)
- **Margin**: ~68%

## Pricing Rationale

### Why $149 for Council (not $99 or $199)?
1. **Anchoring**: $149 is 5x Pro; feels premium but justified
2. **Competitiveness**: ChamberMate is $99-499; we're mid-market
3. **Unit economics**: $149 breaks even with 6 customers (achievable Month 3)
4. **Psychological**: $149 signals "enterprise" better than $99

### Why DOCX only on Council+?
- DOCX is the #1 feature request from governance buyers
- Justifies tier jump from Pro ($29) to Council ($149)
- 90% of our target market needs DOCX
- Creates "must have" vs "nice to have" tiers

### Annual Discount (20% off)?
- Council monthly: $149 × 12 = $1,788
- Council annual: $1,788 × 0.80 = **$1,430**
- Incentivizes annual commitment; improves retention metrics

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low conversion from Free to Pro
- **Mitigation**: Free tier has 5-meeting limit (force trial endpoint)
- **Mitigation**: Free tier excludes governance report (Pro feature)
- **Mitigation**: Email sequence: "3 governance best practices" → link to governance report

### Risk: Churn from Council tier
- **Mitigation**: Monthly governance report reinforces value
- **Mitigation**: API access locks in integrations (switching cost)
- **Mitigation**: Multi-chamber setup (expand to Network tier)

### Risk: Discount pressure from large deals
- **Policy**: "Council at $149, Network custom per seat count"
- **Offer**: Annual discount (20% off) instead of per-seat discount
- **Escalation**: Network tier → enterprise account manager

## Glossary

- **ACV**: Annual Contract Value = revenue over year
- **ARR**: Annual Recurring Revenue
- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost
- **LTV**: Lifetime Value = expected total revenue per customer
- **MRR**: Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **NRR**: Net Revenue Retention (shows expansion revenue)
- **Payback**: Months to recover CAC from customer revenue
- **SOM/SAM/TAM**: Market sizing tiers

# ChamberAI Premium Sales Playbook

## Overview

This playbook enables sales team to convert "discerning chambers" (governance bodies with $150-500/month budgets) to Council tier ($149/mo) and Network tier ($399/mo) subscriptions.

**Target**: 100 Council tier customers + 50 Network tier by Year 1
**Sales motion**: 4-week cycle (discovery → demo → pilot → contract)
**Expected close rate**: 15-20% of qualified prospects

---

## Part 1: Discovery Call Script

**Goal**: 10-minute call to determine if prospect is a good fit for Council tier.
**Use case**: Inbound lead from landing page or cold email.

### Opening (1 min)

> "Hi [Name], thanks for signing up for ChamberAI. I'm [Your Name] with the sales team. I see you're the Executive Director at [Chamber]. I'm calling because a few of your peers at chambers like yours have switched to ChamberAI for governance, and I wanted to see if that might be relevant for you. Do you have 10 minutes?"

### Qualification Questions (7 min)

Ask in order. If they say "no" to #3-5, consider Free tier. If "yes" to most, position Council tier.

1. **How many people on your board/leadership team?**
   - Ideal: 15-50 (Council tier sweet spot)
   - Free: <5
   - Network: >50

2. **How do you currently manage meeting minutes?**
   - Listen for: email, Google Docs, Word documents, no system
   - Red flag: "We use ChamberMate" (incumbent)
   - Win signal: "It's chaotic"

3. **Do you have an auditor or compliance officer who reviews your records?**
   - Council win signal: "Yes, they're very particular"
   - Pro lead: "Yes, but we manage it"
   - Free: "No, we're small"

4. **What would your ideal minutes export look like?**
   - Council signal: "Word format for our legal files" or "signed/dated for audit"
   - Pro signal: "PDF is fine"
   - Free signal: "Just digital storage"

5. **If you could see metrics on how your board is doing (action completion, time to approval, etc.), would that be useful?**
   - Council signal: "Yes, I report to the board on efficiency"
   - Pro signal: "Maybe, but nice-to-have"
   - Free signal: "No, not important"

6. **Are you integrating meeting records with other systems (CRM, legal, finance)?**
   - Council signal: "Yes, we use [Salesforce/Zoho/other]"
   - Pro signal: "We'd like to"
   - Free signal: "No"

### Close Discovery (2 min)

**If Council-tier fit:**
> "Based on what you've told me, I think Council tier would be a great fit. It includes DOCX export, governance analytics, and API access — all things that help with your audit requirements and reporting. Can I schedule a 20-minute demo? I'll walk you through how other chambers similar to yours use it."

**If Pro-tier fit:**
> "I think Pro tier is the right starting point. It gives you governance reports plus Zapier integration, so you can automate some of your workflows. Want to try it free for 7 days?"

**If Free-tier fit:**
> "Let's get you set up on the free tier. No credit card required. You can invite your team, and we can check in after you've run a meeting or two."

## Part 2: Demo Call Flow (Council Tier)

**Duration**: 25 minutes
**Goal**: Show 3 key features, get to "yes" or "let me talk to my team"
**Props**: Live demo account with sample data, governance report

### Setup (2 min)
- Share screen
- Open demo chamber with 3 sample meetings
- "I've set up a sample chamber so you can see exactly how this works"

### Feature 1: Minutes + DOCX Export (8 min)

**Show**:
1. Create a new meeting (date, location, attendees)
2. Add a motion: "Approval of 2024 budget — passed with amendment by John, seconded by Mary"
3. Add action items: "John to send financials to board by March 15"
4. Show draft minutes (collaborative, real-time)
5. **Export to DOCX**: Click "Export" → select "Word" → download file
6. Open the DOCX file — show it has tables for motions, action items, signature line
7. "Notice the approval signature line at the bottom — your board chair can sign this digitally before filing"

**Your talk track**:
> "The reason Council tier includes DOCX is that 90% of the chambers we talk to need to file minutes in Word format — either for your legal system, your auditor, or your corporate governance binder. With ChamberAI, minutes go from voice recording → draft → approval → certified DOCX in one platform. Your auditor gets exactly what they need, and you have an audit trail showing who approved when."

### Feature 2: Governance Compliance Report (8 min)

**Show**:
1. Scroll to "Analytics" → "Governance Report"
2. Show compliance score: 87%
3. Show checklist:
   - ✓ Quorum recorded
   - ✓ All motions have outcome
   - ✓ All action items have owner
   - ✗ Action items not all due-dated
4. Show flags: "2 action items overdue"
5. "This is what you'll show your board president or auditor"

**Your talk track**:
> "One of the things we hear from executive directors is, 'I need to prove to my board that we're governed well.' With ChamberAI, you run a meeting, and at the end of the month, you get a governance report card. It shows your score, it shows gaps (like an action that's overdue), and it shows trends (like 'we've approved motions 15% faster this quarter'). This is gold for your board packet and your audit."

### Feature 3: Real-Time Collaboration (5 min)

**Show**:
1. Open draft minutes in edit mode
2. Show version history: "You can see every change, who made it, when"
3. Click "Rollback" — show how to restore a previous version
4. "Your secretary drafts, you review and make comments, your chair approves — all in real-time, no email chain"

**Your talk track**:
> "Minutes used to be: meeting → secretary types notes → email draft around → collect feedback → reconcile → final version. That's 8 hours. With ChamberAI, it's: recording → AI draft → you polish in 15 minutes → chair approves → done. And because everything is versioned and logged, you have a clear audit trail."

### Trial or Next Step (2 min)

**If they're engaged**:
> "Want to run a real meeting with us next week? We can set you up with a 3-week pilot at $49/month, and then you can decide if Council tier is right for you. Most chambers convert during the pilot."

**If they need to talk to their team**:
> "I totally understand. Why don't I send you a short video recording of this demo, plus a summary of how Council tier compares to Pro? You can review with your board, and let's reconnect end of week."

## Part 3: Objection Handling

### Objection #1: "We don't need DOCX, we use PDF"

**Response**:
> "That's totally fair. A lot of chambers do use PDF. Two things to consider: (1) If you ever need to edit a minutes file — change a date, update an action item — PDF is locked. With DOCX, you can edit freely. (2) Many legal and finance teams expect Word format because they can cross-reference it with other documents. That said, if PDF is perfect for you, Pro tier at $29/month covers that, plus you get governance reports. Would that be a better fit?"

**Outcome**: Likely they're Pro tier, not Council. Adjust accordingly.

---

### Objection #2: "This is too expensive. We have ChamberMate at $99/month"

**Response**:
> "I get it — ChamberMate is the incumbent. Here's the honest comparison: ChamberMate was built in 2004. It doesn't have AI minute drafting, it doesn't have real-time collaboration, and it doesn't have DOCX export. With ChamberAI, you spend the same $149 — maybe $1 more per month — but you cut minutes prep time from 8 hours to 1 hour. That's 7 hours × $30/hour secretary time = $210 value per month. You actually save money. Plus, you get governance compliance reports your auditor will love. Want to run a 3-week pilot to see the time savings yourself?"

**Outcome**: If they bite on ROI math, do pilot. If they're price-anchored to $99, offer annual discount: "$149/month if monthly, $1,430/year if you commit annually." That's 20% off and locks in 24-month value.

---

### Objection #3: "We don't have the budget this year"

**Response**:
> "Totally understand. A few options: (1) You could start with our Pro tier at $29/month — that gets you governance compliance reports without the DOCX and API features. (2) Or, if you can find $150 in your annual budget (you probably spend more on coffee), I can set you up with an annual contract at $1,430/year — that's 20% off the monthly price, and it locks in your governance solution for the full year. Which feels more doable?"

**Outcome**: 70% of the time, you either land Pro tier or annual Council tier (better than free).

---

### Objection #4: "We'll just use Google Docs like we do now"

**Response**:
> "I hear you — Google Docs is free and easy. The challenge is: (1) Google Docs doesn't have an audit trail showing who approved minutes when. If your auditor asks, 'Who signed off on this?' you can't tell them. (2) Your motions and action items live somewhere else — probably email — so you can't cross-reference them. (3) It's hard to automate: no integration with Salesforce if you use that, no way to pipe minutes to your legal system. ChamberAI solves all three. For $149/month, you're really buying audit compliance and time savings. Hard to argue against that. Want to try it for 3 weeks and see if your auditor is happier?"

**Outcome**: Try pilot. If they say no, they're probably Free tier forever; don't push.

---

### Objection #5: "We need to talk to [CFO/Board chair/Executive committee]"

**Response**:
> "Of course. That's actually smart. Here's what I'd suggest: (1) I'll send you a 2-minute video showing the DOCX export and governance report. (2) You can run that by your CFO or board chair in 10 minutes. (3) If they're interested, I'll join a quick 15-minute call to answer any questions. (4) Then, if everyone's on board, we do a 3-week pilot. Does that timeline work?"

**Outcome**: You've removed friction. They'll present to committee. Most will come back and say "yes, let's try it."

**Goal**: Convert pilot to annual contract

Here's why: (1) $1,430/year is 20% cheaper than monthly ($1,788), (2) you lock in your rate for 12 months (we're raising prices in Q3), (3) you get priority support if anything comes up.

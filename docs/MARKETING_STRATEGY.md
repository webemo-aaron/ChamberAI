## FAQ

**Q: How is this different from ChamberMate?**
A: ChamberMate handles membership + payments. ChamberAI specializes in meetings—better minutes, collaboration, decisions. Many chambers use both.

**Q: Can we self-host?**
A: Yes! Full Docker deployment docs included. Cloud version available at $29+/month.

**Q: What if we only have 5 people?**
A: Free tier covers it. No credit card needed, works forever free.

**Q: How do we ensure data security?**
A: Open-source (audit anytime), encrypted storage, your choice of infrastructure.

**Q: Can we integrate with our CRM?**
A: Yes! API available. Common integrations: Zapier, Slack, Google Workspace.

**Q: What's your support like?**
A: Free tier = community support. Paid = email/chat support, 24-48 hr response.

## Problem/Solution Framework

### Section: "The Chamber Meeting Problem"

**Headline**: "How Much Are Bad Meeting Processes Costing You?"

**Problem Flow**:

1. **Before Meeting**
   - Secretary spends 2 hours preparing agenda
   - Finding past decisions for context
   - Sending reminder emails
   - Tracking attendance

2. **During Meeting**
   - Minutes-taker struggling to keep up
   - People talking over each other
   - Decisions get fuzzy
   - Action items mentioned but not tracked

3. **After Meeting**
   - Secretary spends 3 hours cleaning up notes
   - Multiple "what happened?" follow-up emails
   - Action items get lost
   - No historical record for next year's board

**Cost Analysis**:
```
Secretary time: 2 hrs prep + 2 hrs notes + 2 hrs follow-up = 6 hrs/week
× $50/hr (fully burdened) = $300/week
× 50 weeks/year = $15,000/year lost to meetings admin

Plus: Lost decisions, missed action items, compliance gaps
```

# ChamberAI Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for everyone who
participates in this project. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an
open and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in speech and actions.
- Assume good intent; ask questions before escalating disagreements.
- Provide constructive feedback and accept it graciously.
- Be mindful of the impact of your words and actions.
- Respect differing viewpoints, backgrounds, and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind.
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome advances.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to
security@chamberai.dev. Maintainers will investigate and respond promptly and
confidentially.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual's behavior is directed at the project or its community.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these principles when addressing incidents:

1. **Correction**: A private, clear statement of the violation and guidance for future
   behavior.
2. **Warning**: A formal warning and any conditions for continued participation.
3. **Temporary Ban**: A time-bound ban for repeated or severe violations.
4. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community for persistent or egregious behavior.
